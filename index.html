SCHOOL BUS GRAVEYARD

Ashlyn is a loner in high school with no friends. But when a fateful visit to a haunted house causes her and her classmates to see monsters at night, she'll be forced to forge bonds to survive.

ABOUT

"School Bus Graveyard" by Red is a gripping webtoon that explores themes of suspense, horror, thriller and dark fantasy. The story is set in Georgia, with the school bus graveyard as a place where they all spent their times having fun and the place where they felt at home. But that didnt last, cause every night, right at 12.00 am they move to the "Phantom" Dimension to fight for thier lives from the big and dark monsters called the "phantoms" only to be returned back to the human dimension at at the same time...time doesnt work in the Phantom dimension, and as they fight to find a way back to thier normal lives, they form a bond as real friends, and as a found family which gives the readers the comfort amid all the chaos and the plot twists and the trauma...don't forget they were just kids T_T, and they went thorough a lot ;(
